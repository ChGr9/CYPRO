User Acceptance Plan

The web app was tested by the members of our team.
Issues found and fixed:

  • Treasure hunt was displayed twise when you played it again
  • Messages were partially hidden by points (was also pointed by tutor)
  • Questions were not hidden when the session was completed
  • Skipped button appeared when a question couldnt be skipped
  • Answer boxes were not being cleared after submiting the answer
